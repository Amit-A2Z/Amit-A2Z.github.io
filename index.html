The approach

The hard part of AI isn't the model. It's the moment someone uses it.

The problem

Every enterprise is racing to deploy AI on SAP. Most deployments land the model but miss the interface. The AI runs. Nobody uses it. Six months later adoption sits at 15% and the sponsor is defending the invoice.

The insight

The enterprises winning with AI treated the interface as seriously as the model. The model is the engine. The moment someone actually uses it is the product. That is a design problem, not a training problem.

What we do

We prepare the full SAP landscape for AI - data foundations, platform services, process intelligence, agentic orchestration - then design the experience that makes it land. Sixteen years ago we did this for Fiori and mobile. We do it again for AI.
